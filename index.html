The Metal Scoop

A full metal blog made by metal fans for metal fans.

GIMME THAT BOOM – Skindred

A hard-hitting, take-no-prisoners, ragga-metal anthem ‘Gimme That Boom’ deals with the selfish entitlement that’s become so prevalent in this age of instant gratification, based on a frustrating encounter lead singer Benji Webbe had with an over-familiar fan. Source: GrimmGent.com

O.D. – Polyphia

Hailing from the quiet suburbs of Plano, Texas, progressive rock outfit Polyphia are anything but mild-mannered. With a distinctly thought-out and well-orchestrated sound, the quartet pummels out blistering blast beats, and an onslaught of guitar shreds that blends, seamlessly, with melodic grooves and a humble intensity that never wears on the ear. Capitalizing on a “Standard of Excellence”, the band wishes to inspire those who listen with their doctrine of uncompromised work ethic and self-motivated success. Source: SonicHits.com

Fuel – Metallica

Beginning with strong pure vocals to grasp the listeners’ attention, “Fuel” is an energetic and quick-paced track inspired by the adrenaline rush one can receive from speeding through traffic and night-time racing.

Despite a less intense feel throughout Reload, “Fuel” is often regarded by fans as a great candidate for head-banging and high energy in live crowds. Considering the energy of the track, Metallica has featured it in many live performances, as detailed on their website. Source: Genius.com

Silvera – Gojira

 “Silvera” by Gojira is a poignant reflection on personal growth, accountability, and the potential for transformative change within oneself and society at large. The lyrics emphasize the importance of facing adversity with courage and the understanding that individual actions have the power to shape the world. The song’s vivid imagery and powerful refrains serve to drive home the urgency of this message. Soure: LyricsMeanings.com

Schism – TOOL

Tool’s 2001 single “Schism” is a rock song about the breakdown of a relationship and what it takes to rebuild it. It’s about miscommunication, drifting apart, and the constant struggle to stay aligned in a close relationship. Using science and structure as metaphors, it shows how something that once made perfect sense can fall apart when communication breaks down. Source: LyricStories.com
